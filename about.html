About

International Conference on Earth and Environmental Sciences & Technology (ICEEST)” is a platform created by the Faculty of Earth and Environmental Sciences, University of Dhaka. The platform has come to action in 2020 with its first international conference entitled “International Conference on Earth and Environmental Sciences and Technology for Sustainable Development- 2020”. The mission and vision of the ICEEST is to create opportunity for the researchers all over the world to present their works and share their ideas in front of a diverse community. ICEEST values justice, equity, diversity, and inclusion irrespective of gender, race, cast, and ethnicity.
