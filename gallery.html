The first “International Conference on Earth and Environmental Sciences & Technology (ICEEST) - Towards Climate Resilient and Sustainability” was organized with a view to opening the horizon of blue economy, improving the perception of geo-resources, understanding the changing climate and disaster risk in a global context, studying urban life and regional development, analyzing and forecasting the atmosphere, and figuring out the sustainable Delta Management. Hundreds of earth scientists and students enlightened the event with their spontaneous presence in the conference.
