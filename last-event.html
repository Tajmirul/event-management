First ICEEST

First ICEEST

The first “International Conference on Earth and Environmental Sciences & Technology (ICEEST) - Towards Climate Resilient and Sustainability” was organized with a view to opening the horizon of blue economy, improving the perception of geo-resources, understanding the changing climate and disaster risk in a global context, studying urban life and regional development, analyzing and forecasting the atmosphere, and figuring out the sustainable Delta Management. Hundreds of earth scientists and students enlightened the event with their spontaneous presence in the conference.

The Event of First Conference

First ICEEST at a glance,
- Held on 25-30 January 2020 at Hotel Intercontinental Dhaka, Bangladesh.
- Chief Guest: The Honorable President of the Peoples Republic of Bangladesh, His Excellency Mr. Abdul Hamid attended as the Chief Guest in the opening ceremony of the conference.
- Inauguration: The Honorable Minister of Education, Dr. Dipu Moni, MP inaugurated the conference program.
- Total 6 subthemes with 24 thematic areas.
- 73 international participant from 20 countries and 580 local researchers attended in the conference.


- Total 32 sessions were arranged to present 465 scientific articles and 800 conference books were printed and gifted to each participant as a conference material.
- A well-organized field trips were conducted in the world’s largest mangrove forest, the Sundarbans area and another field trip was conducted in the world’s longest sea beach, the Cox’s Bazar area.
- 22 national and international organizations and academic institutions came forward with their financial support for the conference.
- Concluding ceremony: Dr. Hasan Mahmud, MP, Honorable Minister, Ministry of Information was presented as Chief Guest.
